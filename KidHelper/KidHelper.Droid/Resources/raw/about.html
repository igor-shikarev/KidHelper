About

The application is designed to facilitate communication with both nonspeaking children diagnosed with autism, mental retardation, etc. alalia, and adults who are diagnosed with aphasia.

The application is quite simple to use - the parents or the teachers themselves can download the required photo or image, while if desired, and can voice them. The child must select the desired image from the two proposed - for example, "yes" or "no."

The main difference between this application of these - the ability to teach even a mentally retarded child with a "zero", as proposed to select only two familiar objects or characters.

Therefore, the application will be a good assistant for education and child care.
